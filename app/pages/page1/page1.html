JGBoilerplate 2 - Tab 1

Welcome to JGBoilerplate 2!

A JoomGap starter project to create a hybrid mobile app for Joomla! CMS, powered by Cordova/PhoneGap, AngularJS 2 and Ionic 2.

This starter project comes with simple tabs-based layout for apps that are going to primarily use a Tabbed UI.

Take a look at the www/app/ directory to add or change tabs, update any existing page or create new pages.
